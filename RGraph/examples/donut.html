RGraph: Javascript charts and graph library - Donut charts

Formerly the Donut chart was a separate class, though now it's a variant of the Pie chart. This means smaller downloads and a much simpler implementation. Essentially it's a Pie chart with a big hole in the middle. comparisons easier.
